## :icon-question: Bloxstrap How to Use:
1. **Open** the *Bloxstrap Menu*.
2. **Navigate** to `Appearance` >> Switch Style to `Custom` >> `+ New` >>  `Import` >> `Import from file`.
3. **Select the `.zip` you have downloaded**.
4. **Save** and your good to go!
